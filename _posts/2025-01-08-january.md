## Operational Hours

### J-Term Hours

The library is open **Monday through Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.** for the entirety of **J-Term**.

Operating hours will adjust for **Spring Term 2025** starting on **January 27** and will be posted beside the main entrance doors at the beginning of the new term.

---

## Events

### Warm-up Wednesdays

Looking for something to wake up for other than classes?  
Stop by the library every **Wednesday morning during J-Term** to grab **hot chocolate from 8:30–9:00 a.m.** Other refreshments will be available on a limited basis.

---

## Displays

### New Displays Coming Soon!

The library is working on two new displays this month:

- A **Classics** display  
- A **Black Leadership** display  

Both will be located in the **raised mezzanine area** of the library. Visit throughout January to see the displays take shape.

---

## Collections

### New Games and Puzzles Available Now

A broader selection of **games and puzzles** is now available in the **Leisure Reading section** on the first floor.

- Games and puzzles are **for in-library use only** and cannot be checked out.  
- They may be used anywhere in the library, as long as:
  - Games are put back properly after use.
  - Puzzles do not overcrowd study tables or workspaces.

Have a game or puzzle to donate?  
You can **drop donations off with a librarian or at the circulation desk** while the library is open.  
*(Please do not place puzzles or games in the outdoor drop box.)*

---

## Electronic Resources

### New Databases Available

The library has added three **index databases** to expand your research options:

- [**Book Review Digest Retrospective: 1903–1982**](https://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?authtype=ip,uid&custid=s8330781&groupid=main&profile=ehost&defaultdb=brr&scope=site)  
- [**Education Index Retrospective: 1929–1983**](https://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?authtype=ip,uid&custid=s8330781&groupid=main&profile=ehost&defaultdb=eir&scope=site)  
- [**Family & Society Studies Worldwide**](https://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?authtype=ip,uid&custid=s8330781&groupid=main&profile=ehost&defaultdb=flh&scope=site)  

These databases support a **broader range of source searching** and can be accessed from the “Databases” section of the  
[H.C. Hofheimer II Library](https://www.vwu.edu/library/) website.

> **Note:** Index databases list **citations** to relevant sources but do not guarantee that Virginia Wesleyan University holds each item in full text.  
> If full text is not available through VWU, you can request the item via **Interlibrary Loan**.
