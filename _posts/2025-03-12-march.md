## Operational Hours

### Spring Break Hours

Spring Break is right around the corner! As a reminder, Spring Break runs from **March 17 through March 21, 2025**. The library’s operating hours during Spring Break are as follows:

- Monday through Thursday: 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM  
- Friday and Saturday: Closed  
- Sunday: 4:00 PM to 12:00 AM (midnight)

---

## Awards

### Library Research Award

Updated information on the annual Library Research Award is now available. This **$125 cash award** is presented annually at the Spring Honors Convocation to recognize outstanding use of library resources with a project completed during the **2024–2025 academic year**.

The deadline for submissions is **Wednesday, April 23**. More information about the award and the application process can be found [here](https://guides.vwu.edu/help).

## Displays

### Women’s History Month Display

The library’s newest display is for **Women’s History Month**. Featuring Nobel Prize winner Han Kang’s newest title, [*We Do Not Part*](https://vwu.on.worldcat.org/search/detail/1431884036?queryString=we%20do%20not%20part&databaseList=&clusterResults=true&groupVariantRecords=false&bookReviews=off) (2025), and works in feminist thought such as [*In Defense of Witches: The Legacy of the Witch Hunts and Why Women Are Still on Trial*](https://vwu.on.worldcat.org/search/detail/1410835358?databaseList=&queryString=in%20defense%20of%20witches) (2023) by Mona Chollet, the Women’s History Month Display is dedicated to feminist theory and history, activism, and fiction written by notable women.

Come check it out today!

### Black Joy & Resistance

Our **Black Joy & Resistance** display is remaining up for the month of March and has been moved to the shelves outside of the Neil Britton Gallery. Stop by the library to check out titles such as [*The Black Joy Project*](https://vwu.on.worldcat.org/search/detail/1376495515?databaseList=&queryString=the%20black%20joy%20project) (2023) by Kleaver Cruz and [*We Refuse: A Forceful History of Black Resistance*](https://vwu.on.worldcat.org/search/detail/1401907096?queryString=ti%3Awe%20refuse&databaseList=&clusterResults=true&groupVariantRecords=false&bookReviews=off) (2024) by Kellie Carter Jackson.
